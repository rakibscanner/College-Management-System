About


Global School & College mission is to nurture Allah centric youth to overcome today’s challenges with innovative solutions. Global School & College mission serves as the central ideology in crafting all programs and initiatives. It is reinforced in daily, weekly and annual activities. Daily reminders about service to Allah and adherence to Islamic ideology serve to reaffirm the faith and mission of the school. Professional development plans are designed to include the spiritual as well as academic needs of students and teachers. Character education themes are derived directly from the Qur’an and the Sunnah of Prophet Muhammad sallallaahu ‘alayhi wa sallam. Striving to achieve the goals outlined in the mission plays an integral part in the life of Global School & College students, teachers, and support staff.
